Today’s Panchang (Aaj Ka Panchang)

Panchang is the Hindu calendar followed by Vedic astrology, which provides complete detail on each day’s Tithis and auspicious and inauspicious timings. Today’s Panchang on Thanathu Madom Devasthanam is based on Vijay Vishwa Panchang, which is the rarest of Panchang, used by Thanathu Madom Devasthanams for hundreds of years.

Through Daily Panchang, you can get all the information about the time, date, and day to determine the Muhurat for everything. Thanathu Madom Devasthanams suggest people should follow the Day Panchang while doing new work or performing any auspicious event.
