Sacred Heritage

Our Rich Tradition

Thanathu Madom, belonging to the revered Gowthama Rishi Clan, upholds a divine puja tradition that has flourished for over 250 years. Deeply rooted in ancient heritage, it extends its reach to devotees worldwide through astrology services and puja offerings, making Thanathu Madom a blessed sanctuary of eternal grace.

The Thanathu Madom family has had the privilege of serving in many esteemed temples across Kerala, dedicating themselves to preserving authentic Vedic rituals with unwavering devotion and purity. With the blessings of the Guru Parampara, Thanathu Madom extends its spiritual services to devotees across the globe through trusted online astrology consultations and personalized puja offerings.

Brahmashree Dr Harikrishnan Potty O

The Vedic Pujari, Brahmashree Dr. Harikrishnan Potty O, grandson of the esteemed Brahmashree Vasudevan Potty and son of the revered Brahmashree V Omanakuttan Potty, continues this divine pooja tradition with unwavering dedication.

Our Sacred Process at Thanathu Madom

How the Puja is prepared, performed, and shared with the devotee.

At Thanathu Madom, we prepare a dedicated Homa Kundam (sacred fire altar) following the authentic Kerala Tantric tradition, ensuring purity, discipline and scriptural accuracy in every ritual. For each devotee, we perform an individual Sankalpa by clearly reciting the Name, Birth Star (Nakshatra) and Gothra, aligning the homam precisely with their personal intentions and karmic energies.

The ritual includes powerful mantra chanting along with pure Ghee havan, where clarified butter is offered into the sacred fire. Ghee havan is highly auspicious as it purifies the environment, amplifies mantra vibrations, removes negative energies, balances planetary influences and attracts divine blessings for prosperity, peace and overall well-being.
